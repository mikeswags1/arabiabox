Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How much does it cost?
The Android Arabic Box is a $200 one-time purchase. The Linux World Box is $230 for the first year, and the Android Channel Box is $185 for the first year. Both subscription boxes renew for $120 per year after the first year. Every option includes hardware support.
Which boxes require a subscription?
The $200 Android Arabic Box is a one-time purchase with no annual renewal. The Linux World Box is $230 for the first year and the Android Channel Box is $185 for the first year; both renew for $120 per year afterward.
Which channels are included?
The lineup depends on the box. The $200 Android Arabic Box includes some, but not all, Arabic channels plus selected English channels. The $230 first-year Linux World Box adds Arabic and English channels, channels from all countries, movies and TV shows. The $185 first-year Android Channel Box provides the broad live-channel system on Android.
What does the warranty cover?
Every box comes with a full one-year warranty covering the hardware. If anything goes wrong with your device in the first twelve months, we take care of it.
What if I need help setting it up?
Our technical support is available all the time. Whether it's first-time setup, connecting to Wi-Fi, or any question down the road — just reach out and we'll walk you through it.
